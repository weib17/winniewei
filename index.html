Background

I'm currently in my third year of Electrical and Biomedical Engineering at McMaster University, based in Hamilton, Ontario. My work spans two directions that share a common thread: signals that need to be measured, conditioned, and trusted — whether that's low-voltage power distribution on a race car or a tactile sensory substitution device for medical use.

Outside coursework, I build side projects that let me practice the full pipeline — mechanical design, circuitry, firmware, and data — end to end.
